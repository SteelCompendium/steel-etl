*You feint to move your enemies into perfect position.*

| **Melee, Strike, Weapon** |     **Main action** |
|---------------------------|--------------------:|
| **📏 Melee 1**            | **🎯 One creature** |

**Power Roll + Might or Agility:**

- **≤11:** 5 + M or A damage
- **12-16:** 8 + M or A damage; you can swap places with the target
